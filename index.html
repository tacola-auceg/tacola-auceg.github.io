About Us

Tamil Computing Lab(tacola) redefines the way regional languages are manipulated through a machine perspective. Located in the Department of Computer Science, houses state of the art research in the field of Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, Information Extraction/Retrieval for English and regional language in collaboration with the Department of Information Science and Tech.
